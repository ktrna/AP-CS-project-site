Drawing App

Snap Game

I used the programming language Snap! to create my drawing app. The intended purpose of the drawing app is to teach people what the names of colors are and allow people to express their creativity by drawing or by drawing a shape. The app functions by having a “clear” button to clear the screen, several color options to draw freestyle with, and a button to draw the letter A in any of the color options.
